Tortas Fritas

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water or Milk
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Mix flour and salt in a bowl.
  2. Add water or milk gradually, stirring and then gently kneading as you go, until the mixture forms a soft, smooth dough (add more flour if the mixture becomes too wet)
  3. Roll dough out to about 1/4-inch thickness, and use a round cutter to make 3 1/2-inch circles. (Or separate dough into about 10 pieces, shape each piece into a ball, and flatten each ball into a pancake about 1/4 inch thick). Poke a hole in each center with your finger, which prevents them from puffing up.
  4. Add enough vegetable oil (or melt enough shortening or lard) in a small skillet to have a depth of about an inch of fat. Heat until fat sizzles when the dough is added. Fry breads in batches until golden brown on both sides, turning them once.
  5. Drain fried tortas on paper towels and sprinkle with sugar while still hot.
  6. Serve warm
